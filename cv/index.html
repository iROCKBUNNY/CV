Curriculum Vitæ

Summary

With a mixed background in electrical engineering and computer science, and a long-lasting interest in graphic design, Tong Qu enjoys a career in the general areas of computer vision, pattern recognition, image processing, and machine learning.

Brief Biography

Tong Qu is a research and development engineer at State Grid Smart Grid Research Institute (SGRI) working on power quality. He received the master's degree in electronic and information engineering from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) in 2013, and the bachelor's degree in communication engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2012. He was a visiting scholar at Multimedia Computing Lab, Renmin University of China, studying web image retrieval. His postgraduate research at PolyU is focused on human face recognition with low-resolution face images. His research interests include computer vision, pattern classification and recognition, object detection and tracking, signal and image processing, and machine learning.
